How This Works

Every job that qualifies gets exactly one running conversation, not eight separate one-off texts. As the job hits each milestone below, the next template gets added to the same group thread that started with T1.

🏠
PT Only Insurance jobs only This automation only applies to jobs in AccuLynx with a Work Type of Insurance PT. Any other Work Type is out of scope — none of them will receive T1–T8.
📌
All customer communication happens through this group text The customer, salesperson, production, billing, and estimating departments are all in the same group message from T1 onward. This is the support team— customers can reply to it any time and reach the right people, and it's how everyone stays in the loop on what the customer has already been told.
🔗
Every text is auto-posted to AccuLynx, in one running thread After each T1–T8 message sends, the system automatically posts it on that job's AccuLynx file with messages prefixed as [Auto-Text Sent]. The first template for a job creates the message thread on the file; every template after that replies into that same thread, so anyone opening the AccuLynx file sees the full customer text history as well as everyone's replies in one place, in order, without needing access to the texting dashboard itself.
⏰
Texts only send 8:00 AM–8:00 PM Eastern The automation is currently batch-run (someone pastes the AccuLynx CSV export and clicks Run Now) rather than instant/live, and it will not send outside that window. Once this has run for two weeks with no issues, it will be switched over to fully automated — same as TC's setup.
Effective July 29, 2026
Go-Live: New Jobs Only, From Contract/Estimate Signed Onward
●
A job becomes eligible for T1–T8 the day it moves to Contract/Estimate Signed status, on or after 7/29/2026

That status change is also what fires T1 (Welcome) — see below. Jobs that reached Contract/Estimate Signed before 7/29/2026 are not retroactively pulled into the automation and are not eligible to be part of the texting automations.

●
Once a job clears that gate, it stays in for the rest of its lifecycle

T2 through T8 keep firing for that job as its status moves forward (LGIB, completed, invoiced, etc.) — the 7/22 rule only controls whether the job gets pulled into the automation in the first place, not each individual template after that.

T1 — Welcome
Has imagePosts to AccuLynx
Trigger

Fires the day a job's Current Status changes to Contract/Estimate Signed.

Message
Hi {{First Name}}, thank you for choosing Peachtree Roofing & Exteriors! We're excited to get started with you at {{Address}}. Check out the image below for important contact information throughout the process. Text in this group message at any point throughout your project to reach your dedicated team.
Sent as a follow-up image (support-team.png) a few seconds after the text above.
T2 — LGIB Insurance Update
Posts to AccuLynx
Trigger

Fires the day a job's Current Status changes to LGIB (Let's Get It Built). This allows the salesperson to break the good news, but also helps push any hard to reach customers to pay more attention to any salesperson communications.

Message
This is an important message from Peachtree Roofing & Exteriors. We have an update on your insurance claim. Please reach out to {{Primary Salesperson}} at {{Salesperson Phone}} ASAP.
T3 — LGIB Materials
Posts to AccuLynx
Trigger

Fires 2 days after the LGIB status date, prompting the customer to reach out if had difficulties reaching them by getting their attention saying roof has already been schedule — but is skipped if T8's own condition (Work Order already submitted for approval) is also true, so a job doesn't get both templates at once.

Message
This is an important message from Peachtree Roofing & Exteriors. Your roof replacement has been scheduled. Please reach out to {{Primary Salesperson}} ASAP at {{Salesperson Phone}} to choose roof materials if haven't scheduled appointment already.
T4 — Roof Completed
Posts to AccuLynx
Trigger

Fires the day of the job's Completed Date in AccuLynx. This gives the customer an opportunity to voice any concerns directly to all departments if haven't voiced them previously.

Message
Hi {{First Name}}! Congrats on your new roof! Do you have any questions or concerns regarding how your roof replacement went? Feel free to respond to this text or call Production directly at (770) 226-5514. Our Estimating Dept is submitting supplemental documentation to {{Insurance Company}}, so please keep an eye out for any funds released. Next steps: If you haven't had your final walk-through and attic inspection please reach out to {{Primary Salesperson}} to schedule.
T5 — Other Trades Completed
Posts to AccuLynx
Trigger

Fires the day of the job's Current Status changing to Other Trade(s) Completed. This gives the customer an opportunity to voice any concerns directly to all departments if haven't voiced them previously.

Message
Hi {{First Name}}! All work has now been completed with Peachtree Roofing! Do you have any questions or concerns regarding work recently completed? Feel free to respond to this text or call Production directly at (770) 226-5514. Our Estimating Dept is submitting final documentation to {{Insurance Company}}, so please keep an eye out for any funds released.

T7 — Post-Adjuster
Posts to AccuLynx
Trigger

Fires the day after the job's Met With Adjuster Date — but is skipped if T2's condition (Current Status = LGIB) is also true, so a job doesn't get both at once.

Message
Your adjustment appointment with {{Insurance Company}} has completed. It typically takes about 3-5 days for the first insurance estimate to be released post-adjustment. Please make sure to forward any email communication to our estimating department at estimating@peachtreeroof.com, so we can review and assist ASAP to keep your project moving. Feel free to respond to this text at any time with any questions. Thank you for choosing Peachtree Roofing & Exteriors!
